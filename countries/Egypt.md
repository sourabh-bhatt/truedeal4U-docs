# Egypt

## Country to travel:
Egypt

## No. of Travellers:
1

## Documents requirement

### TRAVELLER DETAILS

- **Passport**:
  - Valid passport 6 months from the date of travel.
  - Applicants valid passport is required (Handwritten passport is not allowed).
  - Handwritten Form duly filled and signed.

- **Photographs**:
  - 2 color photos 35 x 45mm, White background, Matt finish, 80% face appearance.

### TRAVEL DETAILS

- **Return air tickets**:
  - Copy of confirmed arrival and departure tickets.

- **Hotel Confirmation**:
  - Hotel confirmation from Cairo or tour confirmation from Egyptian Travel Company.

- **Itinerary**:
  - Day-to-day Itinerary of travel.

- **Authorization letter for single traveller**:
  - If traveling alone, an authorization letter from the local tour operator or DMC is mandatory.
  - Not necessary for married couples.

### FINANCIAL DETAILS

- **Bank Statement**:
  - Original Bank statement of last 6 months with Original Bank seal signature (only blue ink) on all pages (80000/- INR per applicant).
  - The statement needs to be on Bank papers with bank Name.

- **ITR**:
  - If the applicant is either a Partner (or) LLP (or) Director, Business ITR of last 3 years.
  - 3 year ITR (or) Form 16 for employed and 3 year Personal ITR for self-employed.

### CONDITIONAL DETAILS

- **Rent Agreement**:
  - Applicable, if passport is issued in a different jurisdiction from the jurisdiction of visa application.

- **Marriage Certificate**:
  - Marriage Certificate or Marriage Affidavit is required, if the Spouses name is not mentioned at the back of the passport.

- **Staying with friends and family**:
  - A letter from the Invitee, stating the names and passport number's of the applicants.
  - Inviting person's permanent address & contact number.
  - Name/passport number of invitees.
  - Duration/purpose of stay.
  - Copy of ID/passport.

### OCCUPATIONAL DETAILS

- **Employed**:
  - Leave Sanction Letter and No Objection Certificate from employer mentioning the dates of leave sanctioned on company letterhead along with the name, designation, and signature of the authorized signatory.

- **Self-employed**:
  - Cover Letter, with the business letterhead.
  - Business registration certificate; can be any of GST certificate, Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership deed, MOA, etc.
  - Add valid proprietorship proof.

- **Student**:
  - Bonafide certificate and Leave letter from educational institution.
  - Student identity card copy.

- **Retired**:
  - Retirement Proof (OR).
  - Pension slip for the last 3 months.
